## Management System

  * [bitnami.com](https://bitnami.com/) — Deploy prepared apps on IaaS. Management of 1 AWS micro instance free
  * [jamf.com](https://www.jamf.com/) —  Device management for iPads, iPhones and Macs, 3 devices free
